How to Make a Peanut Butter Sandwich

Introduction

This guide will show you how to make a delicious peanut butter sandwich. It's perfect for lunch or a quick snack.

What You Will Need

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Butter knife
  • Plate

Steps to Make It

  1. Place two slices of bread on your plate
  2. Use the butter knife to scoop some peanut butter
  3. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Put the other slice of bread on top
  5. Your sandwich is ready to eat!

Tips

You can add jelly or banana slices to make your sandwich even more delicious.

How to Brush Your Teeth Properly

Introduction

Brushing your teeth is important to keep them healthy. This guide will show you the right way to brush your teeth.

What You Will Need

  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • Water
  • Cup

Steps to Brush Properly

  1. Put a small amount of toothpaste on your toothbrush
  2. Wet your toothbrush with a little water
  3. Brush your teeth in small circles for 2 minutes
  4. Don't forget to brush all sides of your teeth
  5. Spit out the toothpaste and rinse your mouth
  6. Rinse your toothbrush and put it away

Tips

Brush your teeth twice a day - after breakfast and before bedtime.
